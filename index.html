The Challenge

An online education company was losing 2.5 hours per week manually onboarding new students. Each enrollment required sending welcome emails, creating CRM entries, following up with students who hadn't activated accounts, and manually escalating issues to the team.

The Solution

We built a multi-phase automation system that:

  • Automatically sends personalized welcome emails upon payment
  • Creates CRM entries with student information and status tracking
  • Sends automated follow-up reminders to students who haven't created accounts
  • Escalates to human support after multiple automated attempts
  • Generates AI-personalized kickoff emails based on student goals and background

The Results

The client eliminated manual onboarding entirely, saving 15+ hours per week and creating a scalable system that improves the student experience while freeing the team to focus on course development and support.
