The Problem I Keep Seeing

I've worked with teams buried in manual tasks — data entry, chasing follow-ups, copy-pasting between tools, building the same reports every week. Smart, talented people spending hours on work a system could handle forever.

The frustration isn't just the wasted time. It's knowing there's a better way and not having someone to build it. Someone who actually listens, speaks plain English, and delivers something that works — not a sales pitch disguised as a consultation.

That's why I started House of Bachae. To be the person I wished my clients had found sooner.
